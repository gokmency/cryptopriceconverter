Privacy Policy – Instant Crypto Converter

Effective Date: July 29, 2025

Instant Crypto Converter is committed to protecting your privacy. This browser extension does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

What We Collect

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII)
  • We do not track browsing history or web activity
  • We do not log IP addresses or geolocation
  • We do not access login credentials or financial data
  • We do not record user interactions, clicks, or keystrokes
  • We do not access website content

Permissions We Use

  • storage: Saves your selected currency and language preferences locally
  • <all_urls>: Allows the extension to detect crypto values on web pages for real-time conversion

Third-Party Services

We do not use analytics, tracking tools, or advertising platforms. No data is shared or sold to third parties.

Local-Only Functionality

All conversions are processed locally in your browser using live public APIs. No user data is sent or stored externally.
